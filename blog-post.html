Why I Am Leaving Barbering For Tech

I have been a barber for about 11 years now. I am currently in school for coding in hopes to better my life. Here are some reasons why I want to make the switch.

My back hurts. A lot.

Barbering puts a lot of strain on my body as I am constantly standing and putting strain on my neck and shoulders. I also have been in two car accidents in the last few years which has made barbering more difficult.

Here is a list of things that will improve when I have less back pain from work:

  • Long distance cycling
  • Better sleep
  • Less chiropractor visits

I need more money than I make now

I am turning 30 this year and have been thinking a lot more about what I want my future to look like. I realized that I dont want to be working as a barber for the rest of my life.

These are some examples of what I plan to do with a better income:

  • Pay off debt and start a savings & retirement
  • Eventually buy property
  • Take more time off

I want to work from home
